Who We Are

OneHope reflects our commitment to presenting the Living Word of God in an engaging manner to children and youth. Jesus Christ is the singular hope for eternity, and as we introduce Him to the next generation, lives are transformed. We are dedicated to the Church and firmly believe that effective, outcome-based ministry for children and youth requires the long-term spiritual support that only the local church can provide.

Vision

Vision 2033 is OneHope’s strategic plan to catalyze a movement that engages every child in every generation with Jesus through His Word.
Vision 2033 is essentially split into two phases. The first is to reach every child in this generation and equip the Church to reach their communities. We prayerfully believe this could happen by the year 2033. We’re believing 2 billion children can be reached with God’s Word between 2023 and 2033. The second phase is the ongoing work to reach every new generation. Even after the year 2033, there’s still ministry to be done!

Mission

To bring the Word of God to every Child in South Africa, through various partnerships with the local church, communities, and schools, in partnership with One Hope.

Our Method

We dedicate significant effort and resources to research and develop world-class materials for children and youth. We place a high value on research because it helps us align our theological development with the heartfelt needs of young people in ways that are contextually appropriate and relevant.
